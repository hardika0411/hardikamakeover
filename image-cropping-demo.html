Click the buttons below to see different cropping effects on your slider images:

Cropping Options Explained:

  • Smart Crop: Focuses on the upper 25% of the image, ideal for portraits
  • Face Focus: Centers on the upper 30% of the image for face shots
  • Portrait: Optimized for portrait photography (upper 20%)
  • Landscape: Standard center cropping for landscape images
  • Directional Focus: Top, Bottom, Left, Right focus points
  • Cover: Scales image to cover entire container (may crop)
  • Contain: Scales image to fit entirely within container
  • Fill: Stretches image to fill container exactly
  • Scale Down: Same as contain but never scales up
